Rules of the Simon

  1. Press any key to start the game
  2. Click on the box highleted
  3. Everytime you click the right box, a following box will be highlighted
  4. Follow the right pattern of the box highleted in order.
  5. After every successful click on the pattern you will be upgraded to the next level
  6. Any single wrong click in the patter will get your Simon killed.
